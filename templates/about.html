Who We Are

We're creators, wanderers, and tech enthusiasts who believe Sri Lanka deserves a platform that truly captures its magic. From ancient temples to hidden waterfalls, we're obsessed with the untold side of travel on this extraordinary island.

Our Mission

To unlock the full potential of travel in Sri Lanka by blending community knowledge with rewarding exploration. We want every traveler — local or foreign — to feel like they have a local best friend in their pocket.

Our Vision

A world where travel is immersive, respectful, and local-first. We envision Expearls as the go-to gateway for discovering authentic Sri Lankan stories, places, and people — responsibly and joyfully.

What We're Building

  • A community platform for discovering real local experiences
  • A gamified reward system — earn points for every check-in and contribution
  • Curated trails and challenges across all nine provinces
  • A platform that gives back to the places and people it features

Expearls is a community-powered travel platform for exploring Sri Lanka. Users can discover places, check in, earn points and badges, follow curated trails, and contribute their own local knowledge to help others explore the island authentically.

You earn points by checking in at places (with optional photo proof for bonus points), adding new places, writing reviews, completing trails, and finishing challenges. Badges are unlocked automatically when you hit milestones — like visiting 5 places or completing your first trail.

Yes — Expearls is completely free to join and use. Discovering places, checking in, earning rewards, and browsing trails all cost nothing. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core experience will always be free.

After logging in, click "Add Place" from the navigation. Fill in the name, description, location coordinates, category, and an optional photo. Your submission goes to our review team and, once approved, earns you 50 points and appears publicly on the platform.

A Trail is a curated collection of places linked into a journey — like "Colombo City Walk" or "Hill Country Waterfalls". When you check in at every place on a trail, you complete it and earn a bonus reward. Anyone can create and share their own trails.

Check-ins have three trust tiers — Unverified, Likely Verified, and Verified. Uploading a photo and sharing your GPS location at the time of check-in increases your trust score and earns bonus points. Our system also detects reused photos to keep things fair.

Yes. You can request account deletion at any time by contacting us at expearlsadmin@gmail.com. We'll remove your personal data within 30 days, though some anonymised content may be retained as required by our policies.

If you spot an error on a place listing or a review that violates our community guidelines, email us at expearlsadmin@gmail.com with the place name and details. Our moderation team reviews all reports promptly.

We take privacy seriously. We do not sell your data to third parties, do not use commercial tracking cookies, and you control what is publicly visible on your profile (location, email, phone). See our full Privacy Policy below for details.

The best way to reach us is by email at expearlsadmin@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 48 hours on business days.
